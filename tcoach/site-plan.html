Home page

Hello! My name is Tara Billman and I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Home and Family Life from BYU and a Certified Life Coach Credential through the International Coaching Federation.

Before my husband and I started our family, we never imagined we would have a child with Tourette Syndrome and that our other three children would also end up with at least one physical or mental health diagnosis.

After our oldest daughter was diagnosed with TS in 2013, I struggled for years trying to control things I couldn't control and at times felt hopeless and defeated. I also struggled to make decisions about things I could control. I didn't know how to let myself feel or process feelings so I stuffed them, and I didn't make self-care a priority.

I can trace the beginnings of my battle with anxiety to around this time period. Constantly worried about the future of my children and what they were going through. Always in fight or flight mode. It was exhausting.

Then in 2021 I discovered life coaching…

  • I learned to recognize and acknowledge what was actually in my control.
  • I learned how to feel and process my feelings.
  • I learned to prioritize self-care.
  • I learned that my thoughts affect my results.
  • I discovered my core values and strengths.
  • I learned how to let some things go, like what other people think or say.
  • And I learned how to trust myself again.

As a result of these breakthroughs…

  • I am willing to be more vulnerable and admit when I need help.
  • I can better manage my feelings, including the anxiety.
  • I am able to choose which thoughts I hold onto and let go of the others.
  • I feel better because I'm taking better care of myself.
  • I am able to make decisions more easily, based on my values.
  • I am more present with my family.
  • And I am able to show up as a parent the way that I want to, with confidence and peace.

Now I know exactly how to support and guide you as you navigate the life changes that come with your child's TS diagnosis.

If you are a mom whose child has been diagnosed with Tourette Syndrome, I want to let you know that I have been where you are. I get you. I see you and I feel you.

And I have created a coaching package that is tailor made just for you!

Life Coaching offers solutions to a lot of things that YOU struggle with as you try to help alleviate your child's suffering.

Program page

Are you tired of watching your child suffer from the effects of Tourette Syndrome and feeling powerless to help them? Do you long to feel confident and peaceful when it comes to parenting your child?

If so, this program is for you!

I see you mom…

  • Your child has been diagnosed with Tourette Syndrome and you don't know how to help them.
  • You find yourself Googling all things related to the diagnosis.
  • Your friends and family and the school think your child should be able to control their tics and they try to give you parenting advice.
  • You find yourself waking up at two in the morning worrying about how you are going to keep it all together and how to best help your child while showing up for your other children too.
  • You wish you were connected to other women who have gone down this road and could be a support to you right away, but you don't know where to find these people.
  • You are having a hard time getting the doctor to listen.
  • No one gets you.
  • People say they are sorry and to let them know what you need, but YOU don't even know what you need, let alone have the ability to delegate anything to anyone else.>

What you will get with this program:

  • You will gain clarity around how to parent your child with TS.
  • You will feel more confident in decisions you are making about your child's treatment.
  • You will learn to manage your thoughts and emotions in a way that gets the results you want in your life.
  • You will feel more centered and balanced as you parent your other children.
  • You will learn how to find joy and fun in life.
  • You will feel more confident in communicating with friends, family and other people who interact with your child.
  • You will learn how to establish strong boundaries with other people without feeling guilty.
  • You will have more clarity on how to balance other areas of your life.
  • You will have more peace, be able to see your strengths and live a more intentional life.
  • You will learn how to take better care of yourself so you can fill your cup and give to those you love.

You might be asking yourself, how do I KNOW that this is for me?

  • This is for you if you feel alone, like no one can possibly understand what you're going through.
  • This is for you if you are feeling frustrated, hopeless, lost or defeated.
  • This is for you if you are struggling to make decisions about your child's care.
  • This is for you if you are trying to juggle multiple responsibilities and you are struggling to keep up in the chaos.
  • This is for you if you've been in survival mode for way too long.
  • This is for you if you aren't taking care of yourself and feel burnt out.
  • This is for you if you want to connect with a mom who can relate.
  • But most importantly, this is for you if you want to feel more confident and peaceful in parenting your child.
  • There is a way to do more than just survive being a parent, YOU CAN THRIVE!
  • You can experience a shift beginning with the very first session, and by the time you complete the program you will be empowered with mindset tools and skills to parent with confidence and peace.

Program FAQ

What is life coaching anyway? A life coach is essentially a thinking partner. I guide you to using your own creativity and resources to see beyond any blocks you might have and solve your own problems. I do this through asking powerful questions, reflecting, observing and summarizing to help you clarify and evaluate your thinking. I hold space for you to explore the gap between where you are now and where you want to be, and provide mindset tools and skills to help get you there. Your job as the client is to be vulnerable and honest, willing to explore what isn't clear about yourself, others, or a situation. Also to be willing to put in some work in between sessions because that is where the real growth happens!

What does a private life coaching call look like? The weekly calls will be 60 minutes in length. We will spend time addressing your main challenges and helping you get clarity and tools to navigate them. A lot of shifts happen on these calls so come p repared to have your mind blown.

My plate is full and I just want someone to talk to. Is homework required? No. If I offer something as “homework” during a session it is always optional. The action steps YOU develop and commit to during a session may be all you need to move forward.

How will the monthly payments be charged to my account? You will be emailed a bill every 30 days that you can pay by credit or debit card.

About page

Who is Tara?

Tara Billman has always been interested in personal growth and development. She has a Bachelor's degree in Home and Family Life from BYU and is an ICF Certified Coach.

She lives in Idaho with her husband and 3 of her 4 daughters. Raising her daughters has been a path of tremendous growth for Tara as one of her daughters has Tourette Syndrome and scoliosis, two have anxiety, two have a bleeding disorder and one has asthma.

Tara understands the challenges many women encounter to navigate their children's mental and health diagnosis. Her mission through her Coaching Business is to empower moms with skills and mindset tools so they can thrive in motherhood while finding confidence and peace along the way.
